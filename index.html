Visit Raška

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant town in the centre of Europe.

Top four activities to try in Raška

Monastery of Gradac

There are many historical places in Raška. Such as Monastery Gradac (XIII century), Old Pavlica (XI century), New Pavlica (XIV century). On Kopaonik, there are the remains of an early Christian basilica (III or IV century) at an altitude of 1,800 meters.

Swim in thermal water

Jošanička Banja is popular health spa since Roman period on the slopes of Kopaonik. It has watar with a temperature up to 88°C (190°F). Open poola are avilable through all year.

Kopaonik ski resort

Kopaonik is the the most popular Serbian ski center. There are 25 ski lifts with capacity of 32,000 skiers per hour. Snow season lasts from November to April, while there are around 200 sunny days.

Paragliding

The hilly mountainous region of Raška, favorable wind currents and many sunny days, opportunities for all kinds of flights for beginners and experts. Raška has been the location of the European Championships.
